# Executive Summary
## Transport Management System - Commercial Proposal

---

## Project Overview

A comprehensive **Transport Management System (TMS)** designed for logistics and transportation companies, featuring complete order management, LR processing, GST-compliant billing, and customer relationship management.

---

## Key Features at a Glance

### Master Data Management
- **5000+ Customer Records** with GST details
- Multi-branch operations support
- Vendor and transporter management
- Vehicle type management
- State and city master (India)

### Operations Management
- Order/Enquiry tracking (Open → Confirmed → Completed)
- LR (Lorry Receipt) generation with auto-numbering
- Real-time status updates
- Route and vehicle assignment

### Financial & Billing
- GST-compliant invoicing (CGST/SGST/IGST)
- Automatic freight calculation
- Multiple charge types (loading, unloading, hamali, statistical)
- Print-ready bills and LR documents

### Security & Access
- Role-based access (Admin/User)
- Secure authentication
- Branch-level data segregation
- Audit trail capability
